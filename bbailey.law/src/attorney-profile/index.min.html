Blake Bailey

A Texas native himself, Blake Bailey has made a career defending the rights of his fellow Texans. As well as being a local, Mr. Bailey is also a family man and proud father of 4 children. He understands what makes our community tick and cherishes the core principles and values that many of us hold near and dear to our heart.

His prestigious record in the courtroom speaks for itself. He has tried to conclusion close to 200 jury trials, and has an extremely high success rate. Mr. Bailey has been awarded as much as $20,000,000.00 in a single jury verdict. He is a champion of the rights of those injured or killed by negligence or unreasonably dangerous products. He is also an expert in defending those who are victims of bad faith on the part of insurance companies. Just like his commitment to his family and this community, his commitment to excellence and his clients is second-to-none.

In addition to the Bailey Law Firm, Mr. Bailey acts “Of Counsel” with The Martin Walker Law Firm and is also a distinguished lecturer and publisher. He has been invited to speak at several universities all around the world, and has written numerous expert publications.
