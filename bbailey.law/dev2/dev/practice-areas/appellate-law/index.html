Appellate Law

Bringing appellate cases to action requires a high level of proficiency on the part of your attorney. Appellate counsel is tasked with the duty of persuading judges rather than a juries, which can prove difficult for attorneys without years of experience in an appeals process. Appeals can happen in several different areas of the law, so it is imperative that you consider the specifics of your case and choose your appellate lawyer accordingly. Typically, a trial attorney who has experience in appellate law will channel that experience into a certain area of the law. For instance, Mr. Bailey’s focus within appellate law lies in the area of personal injury including automobile accidents, product liability, medical malpractice and insurance bad faith to name a few.

Blake Bailey is admitted to practice in all of the Texas Courts of Appeals, the Texas Supreme Court, the 5th Circuit Court of Appeals, and the United States Supreme Court. Often times, an individual will need his or her case handed over from their trial lawyer to an appeals attorney. Blake’s 4 decades of experience in both gives him a unique advantage, and puts him in a position to keep the opposing counsel from improperly presenting your case to the judge.
